About Likho

Likho is a note-taking service offered by Codepotro. The service also includes lots of simple and cool features. Likho is available as a mobile app for Android. The app offers a variety of tools for taking notes, including text and images. The interface allows for a multi-column view. Notes can be color-coded, and labels can be applied for organization. Likho never use internet but it will use after the next update so that user can sync.
